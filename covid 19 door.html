Abstract

"As a result of the current COVID-19 pandemic situation, it is imperative that every human being take precautions such as wearing a mask, keeping a distance, sanitizing their hands, and not touching anything unnecessary. A prototype of an automatic door control system with a body temperature sensor and counter is designed to limit the direct physical contact to the conventional door knobs, also preventing the spread of COVID-19.The components used for the project are Arduino Uno, Passive Infrared Sensor, MLX90614 Temperature Sensor, 16x2 LCD, Servo motor, and LEDs. The door gets opened when any human movement is detected near the door as well as it detects its temperature and the presence of a mask with the help of various tools and technologies."
